About The Photographer

Sam Bruce is a photographer known for his work capturing photos at church, youth group, and other local events. More recently, his phototography has focused on travel as he captures various locations around Georgia.

From capturing events at church to street photography, Sam has gained valuable exposure in multiple avenues of photography with over three years of experience.

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, Sam continues to explore new techniques and push the boundaries of photography. When not behind the camera, Sam enjoys traveling, hiking, reading, watching movies and spending time with family.
